# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

This project follows a simple code of conduct to keep the community respectful and productive.  
本项目遵循以下行为准则，以保持社区的尊重与高效协作。

---

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.  
我们承诺为所有参与者提供无骚扰的协作环境。

---

## Expected Behavior / 期望行为

- Be respectful and considerate / 尊重他人、保持礼貌  
- Assume good faith / 以善意对待他人  
- Give constructive feedback / 提供建设性的反馈  
- Focus on the project goals / 聚焦项目目标  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior / 不可接受的行为

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination / 骚扰、威胁或歧视  
- Personal attacks or insults / 人身攻击或侮辱  
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberate disruption / 恶意刷屏、捣乱、破坏协作  
- Sharing private information without consent / 未经同意泄露他人隐私  

---

## Reporting / 反馈与举报

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer:  
如果你遇到或目睹不当行为，请联系维护者：

- Email / 邮箱: **llzgd@outlook.com**

---

## Enforcement / 执行方式

Maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal.  
维护者可采取适当措施，包括警告、临时限制或永久移除。

---

Thank you for helping keep this community welcoming.  
感谢你共同维护友好的社区环境。
